Rythu Bharosa Latest News 2025: Status, Beneficiary List, and Mobile App

The Telangana state government has shared the latest updates about the Rythu Bharosa scheme for 2025. They recently announced the release of the first installment of financial help for farmers. Farmers in Telangana who applied for the scheme can check their application status or the beneficiary list online. This makes it easy for farmers to access information from home without visiting government offices.

Rythu Bharosa Latest Update

The Telangana government will start giving financial help to farmers from 26th January 2025. In the first installment, farmers will receive INR 6,000. This money will help farmers buy farming tools and improve their crops. In total, farmers can get up to INR 15,000, while farmworkers can receive INR 12,000. Only farmers living in Telangana can apply for this scheme.

Rs 569 Crore Transferred Under the Scheme

On the first day, the Telangana government transferred INR 569 crore to farmers. More than 4 lakh farmers from 577 villages received this money. Farmers who have not received their share yet will get it before 31st March 2025.

How to Check Rythu Bharosa Status Online

  1. Go to the official website.
  2. Click on the “Check Status” option.
  3. Fill in the required details and upload the necessary documents.
  4. Review your details and click on “Submit”.

Offline Process

  1. Visit your nearest bank branch.
  2. Speak to the bank official and explain your request.
  3. Provide the information they ask for.
  4. The official will give you the status of your application.
