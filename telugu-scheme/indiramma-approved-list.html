Indiramma Approved List 2025

The Telangana government has shared the Indiramma Approved List for 2025. This list includes the names of people who are selected to get help from the government. Only those who meet all the rules and conditions will get the benefits. This list is available online, so people don’t need to visit any office. They can check the list from their home.

What is Indiramma Indlu?

Indiramma Indlu is a plan by the Telangana government to help people build houses. The government wants to help people who don’t have a home. A total of 3,500 houses will be built in 119 areas of the state. Each selected person will get INR 5 lakh as financial help. The money will be given in four parts directly into their bank accounts.

Why is the Indiramma Approved List Important?

The Indiramma Approved List is shared to let people know if they are chosen to get the benefits. Only those who meet all the conditions will find their names on the list. This plan will help poor people who don’t have a home to live a better life.

Who Can Apply?

  • You must live in Telangana permanently.
  • You must be poor or belong to the middle class.
  • You should not already have a permanent house.
  • You must not be registered under any other housing scheme.

Who Cannot Apply?

  • If you got help for housing from the government after 1995, you cannot apply.
  • If you already own a proper house, you are not eligible.
  • If you are a government employee, you cannot apply.

Benefits of the Indiramma Approved List

  • The government received 80 lakh applications for this scheme.
  • This plan will reduce the number of homeless people in Telangana.
  • Selected people will get INR 5 lakh to build a house.
  • It will help poor people live a better life.

How to Check the Indiramma Approved List

Using Aadhaar Number

  1. Visit the official website: indirammaindlu.telangana.gov.in.
  2. Click on “Application Search.”
  3. Select the Aadhaar option.
  4. Enter your Aadhaar number and click “Go.”
  5. The approved list will appear on your screen.

Using Mobile Number

  1. Go to the official website.
  2. Click on “Application Search.”
  3. Select the Mobile Number option.
  4. Enter your mobile number and click “Go.”
  5. The list will appear on your screen.

Using Application Number

  1. Open the official website.
  2. Click on “Application Search.”
  3. Select the Application Number option.
  4. Enter your application number and click “Go.”
  5. The approved list will appear on your screen.

Using Ration Card Number

  1. Visit the official website.
  2. Click on “Application Search.”
  3. Select the Ration Card Number option.
  4. Enter your ration card number and click “Go.”
  5. The approved list will appear on your screen.
