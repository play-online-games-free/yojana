JEE Mains 2025 Admit Card for January 22, 23, and 24 - Direct Link

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has officially released the admit cards for the JEE Mains 2025 Session 1 examinations. Candidates who have registered can now download their admit cards directly from the official website: jeemain.nta.nic.in.

Steps to Download JEE Mains 2025 Admit Card

Follow these steps to access and download your admit card:

  1. Visit the official website: jeemain.nta.nic.in.
  2. Click on the link that reads, ‘Admit Card for JEE (Main) 2025 Session-1 (22, 23, 24 January 2025)’.
  3. A new login page will open.
  4. Enter your credentials, such as Application Number and Password or Date of Birth.
  5. Click on the "Submit" button.
  6. Your admit card will appear on the screen. Download it and take a printout for future reference.

JEE Mains 2025 Exam Dates and Shifts

  • Session 1 Dates: January 22, 23, 24, 28, and 29, 2025.
  • Shifts for Paper 1 (B.E./B.Tech):
    • First Shift: 9 AM to 12 noon
    • Second Shift: 3 PM to 6 PM
  • Papers 2A (B.Arch) and 2B (B.Planning):
    • Exam Date: January 30, 2025
    • Single Shift: 3 PM to 6:30 PM

Important Information

The JEE Mains 2025 Session 1 exam will be conducted across multiple cities in India and 15 international locations. Candidates should carefully verify all details on their admit card, such as name, application number, exam center, and instructions, before appearing for the exam.
